Floods

Floods occur due to heavy rains, river overflow, or dam breaks. They can cause loss of life, displacement, and property damage.

Earthquakes

Earthquakes are sudden shaking of the ground caused by tectonic movements. They damage infrastructure and can trigger tsunamis or landslides.

Heatwaves

Heatwaves are prolonged periods of excessive heat. They impact health, agriculture, and water supply, especially in vulnerable regions.

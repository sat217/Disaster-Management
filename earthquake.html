📌 Reasons for Earthquake

  • Tectonic Plate Movements: Earthquakes happen when plates of the Earth's crust collide or slide past each other.
  • Fault Line Activity: Sudden slips along faults in the Earth's crust release stored energy.
  • Volcanic Activity: Magma movement beneath volcanoes creates tremors in nearby regions.
  • Human -Induced Causes: Mining,deep drilling,or large dams can trigger man-made earthquakes.
  • Isostatic Adjustment: Shifts due to melting glaciers or heavy sediment loads cause crustal readjustment.

🛡️ Safety Precautions

  • Drop,cover & Hold On:Get down,take cover under sturdy furniture and hold on until shaking stops.
  • Stay Indors:Do not rush outside while shaking;protect your head & necks.
  • Stay away from hazards:Keep away from windows,mirrors,tall shelves or anything that can fall.
  • If outside:Move to open ground away from trees,poles,buildings.

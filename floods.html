📌 Reasons for Floods

  • Heavy rainfall: Monsoon rains in India (June-September) are a major trigger for flooding across states like Bihar,Asam,Kerala &West Bengal
  • River overflow: Rivers exceed their carrying capacity & spill over banks.
  • Dam failure: Sudden release of water due to a dam break or emergency discharge.
  • Poor urban drainage: Encroachment pf wetlands,blocked drains,and concreate surfaces reduce water absorption

🛡️ Safety Precautions

  • Move to higher ground immediately.
  • Avoid walking or driving through floodwaters.
  • Keep emergency kits ready (food, water, medicines).
  • Listen to official warnings and alerts.

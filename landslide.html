📌 Reasons for Landslides

  • Heavy Rainfall: Excessive rain saturates the soil,reducing its stability and causing slope failure.
  • Earthquakes: Ground shaking loosens rocks and soil,triggering landslides.
  • Deforestation: Cutting trees removes root support ,weakening slopes.
  • Construction & Mining: Human activities like road building & mining destabilize land.

🛡️ Safety Precautions

  • Move to higher ground: Run to a safe,elevated area immediately.
  • Avoid rivers & slopes:Stay away from flowing water or unstable ground.
  • Protect your head:Use arms,bags,or helmets against falling debris.
  • Listen to warnings:Follow official instructions and evacuation orders.
  • Stay alert for afterslides:Secondary landslides often follow the first.
